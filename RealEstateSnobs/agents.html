Agents

What kind of agent are you?

Agents who want 100% commission

  • Only pay one fee and it includes the FMLS fee.
  • At closing, only pay 0.0024% of the sales price.
  • For Example: $300,000 SP at closing means you pay $720.00 (this includes your FMLS fee of 0.0012%) and your comission at closing to take home would be $8280.00.

Agents who work well and thoroughly on GAR contracts and forms

  • We only use GAR forms.
  • Agents must have a checklist to make sure that all forms are included and submitted to receive pay at close.
  • Know your forms and know the laws.

Agents who are members of a Board

  • You are required to be a member of a board.
  • You must be active or join a board once you come onboard.

Agents who want to work virtually and/or from home

  • We work virtually from home and our own office space.
  • We are a virtual firm.

Agents looking for low to no monthly fees

  • You can pay your fees annually.
  • You can also do a monthly auto draft of $40.00 per month. ($20.00 for GAMLS, $10.00 for email and website content, $10.00 for E&O Insurance)
