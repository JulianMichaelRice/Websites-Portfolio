Land Areas

SCIPULOR


Teleporter: Medusa Teleporter

Coords: (527, 678)

Danger Level 31: 1660 HP

Stab: 66% Cut: 17% Impact: 17%


* VERY BEST Scipulor Daily Mission Location

* Excellent Mining - High Mark Up % Ores and EnMatters

* Chronicle Armor (L)

* ESI (Empty Skill Implant)

* Corrosive Attack Nanochip 6 (L)

* Tier 3 Components

* Liver Oil

Deino, the most beautiful tropical island in all of Entropia is situated directly South of the Medusa Teleporter. The island is known for it's thick and pure Scipulor spawns. If you have a Daily Scipulor Mission this is the single best place to come and work on it. Deino Island is home to hidden waterfalls, small ponds beaming with freshwater fauna and sandy beaches that welcome any traveler's tired feet.

 

 

ATROX


Teleporter: NorthWest of Nate Valley TP

Coords: (252, 360)

Danger Level 29: 1460 HP

Stab: 23% Cut: 36% Impact: 41%


* #1 Medium Sized Atrox spawns on Calypso

* Supremacy Armor (L)

* ESI (Empty Skill Implant)

* Electric Attack Nanochip 5 (L)

* Tier 4 Components

* Tier 3 Components

* Liver Oil

Atrox Paradise contains medium sized Atrox with excellent spawn rates. OLA#02 is just North of the Nate Valley teleporter. You'll experience a unique landscape with alien-like flora and blue skies above. Atrox are a staple of Calypso, a classic Stab/Cut/Impact mob that provides many opportunities to hunters and a chance to score it big with their vast loot table.

COMBIBO n' BERYCLED STALKERS


Teleporter: Shinook Jungle - Go East!

Coords: (290, 512)

Danger Level 12: 560 HP and 390 HP

Cut: 50% Impact: 50%


* ONLY Combibo Stalker LA on Calypso

* Super HIGH MU% Mobs! Redulite in the ground! Drop a probe ;)

* Paint Can (Orange)

* Short Midastree Board

* Tier 1 Components

* Nova Fragments

* Basic Leather Extractor

If you've got a daily mission on Berycled or Combibo there is no better place to go than OLA#30. Shinook Jungle TP is within walking distance of this scenic purple paradise, and home to some of the very highest MU% mobs on Calypso. Below your feet is Redulite, one of the rarest and highest MU% resources in the universe! Shinook Jungle has a FULL SERVICE station loaded with terminals, auctioneers, storage containers and has become a highly trafficked "hang out" spot because of the excellent hunting and mining

NECONU


Teleporter: That Freaking Cold Place

Coords: (188, 515)

Danger Level 21: 900 HP

Cut: 50% Impact: 50%


* Calypso's BEST KEPT SECRET! #1 Neconu spawns!

* Super HIGH MU% Mobs!

* Tier 7 Components

* Angel (L) Armor

* Animal Heart Oil

* Cryogenic Attack Nanochip 9 (L)

* Empty Skill Implant (ESI)

With the addition of its very own Teleporter, That Freaking Cold Place is a newly rediscovered gem on Calypso. It's popularity began to surge late 2019 and early 2020 because its the most accessible Neconu Land Area on Calypso. Neconu is grinded primarily for their High MU% loot. This creature drops one of the best base oils in the game, Animal Heart Oil. It also has a common drop of Fine Wool, Angel Armor (L), and Tier 7 Components. This is truly a spectacular mob for any mid-level hunter to grind
