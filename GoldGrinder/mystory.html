About Entropia Gold


My Story

EntropiaGold was created by me, Rachel MsPudding Hawkins, in the Fall of 2018, but my story starts long before that. MsPudding was born in January, 2011 on Calypso. I began my EU career as a trader in Twin Peaks, purchasing Ores and EnMatters and helping move them on the in-game auction house. My trading career did not last, it was too boring! It was when I discovered the actual game that I began having real fun...

I quickly began to work on my Argonaut Iron Mission, grinding away hour after hour just North of Twin Peaks. It wasn’t long before I experienced that incredible, and unmistaken feeling of those swirling golden lights, showered by an 8k Argonaut Young! What an exciting shock that was!

From there I mined, crafted, and explored the vast universe and planets MindArk and planet partners had to offer. There were creatures five times my size that I vowed one day I would kill. There was armor so beautiful and weapons so powerful, that I only dreamed of the day I would one day own them

More than anything though, deep down, my biggest goal was to one day become a Land Area Owner


In many ways this game felt like the Wild West, survival of the fittest, and has been one of the most difficult games I continue to learn. I chipped out in 2012, and later returned in 2013. I have stayed active since then with zero plans of ever chipping out again. I am here to stay, baby! I love this place, and most of all I love the people

I avoid drama, and as a result have refrained from joining any particular society. I am friends with anyone who wants to be. I admire the many people dedicated to the soc, and dedicated to helping others, and maybe one day I’ll find my place. I consider the community at large my soc. I can proudly say that in all my time in game, I have only ever had to add 2 people to my ‘mute list’. That speaks volumes to the great character of Entropians

I’ve uninstalled other games in minutes because of the toxic and abusive communities, but not Entropia. Our community is special. There have always been numerous people willing to help me out since day one. There was never any shortage of eager people willing to point me in the right direction or spark up an interesting conversation

My avatar accomplishments are modest compared to some, but they are wholly mine. I am proud of what I’ve become. I’ve come from organizing Opalo Hunts in Twin peaks to reaching over 330 natural HP and 520k skills. I’ve placed 1st in several Mayhem’s I’ve competed in, and seek to compete in more

I wanted to explore every facet of Entropia. I wanted to learn the game for myself. I wanted to create my own success, and share my experience with others. Entropia is a game unlike any other. I have found some of the best people I know in Entropia. The challenge is what drove me, the opportunity is what excites me, and the community is what keeps me here

Lootius Mayhem: Episode One launched in the Summer of 2020, and thanks to the support of the community it was the most successful player run event in the history of Entropia! 65,000 PED was awarded to 15 different participants over a 35 day competition.

With your support and encouragement, I hope to continually tweak and improve the mechanics and experience of Lootius Mayhem and my other events. I rely on your feedback and ideas to continually improve and sharpen my tools. Together we are strong

EU’s soul is in all of us. It’s my goal to help you Rediscover Entropia by capturing that excitement and sense of exploration and accomplishment once again
