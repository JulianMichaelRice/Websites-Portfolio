5 Progressive Jackpots

For every Global/HOF on any of the EntropiaGold land areas progressive jackpots will grow larger, until they are triggered and won. After a jackpot is won, it will continue growing until it is won again, this cycle always repeats.

There are 4 mob specific jackpots that are filled and won by hunting specific mobs on specific land areas. There is one general jackpot that is filled and won by hunting any of the mobs on any of the land areas. You’re always eligible to win from at least two different jackpots hunting on any of my land areas. See how the jackpots are filled and triggered below:
