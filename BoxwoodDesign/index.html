About Boxwood Design

Over the course of a decade, Boxwood Design has built a solid reputation in the field of interior design and space planning. Fusing form and function, the spaces they create revitalize and inspire in their decadent simplicity. The creative force behind the firm, Helen Brach, founded this company with a consumer-centric vision. Their clients’ needs and individuality are expressed and guided towards a timeless bespoke creation.

The group has established a firm presence with their ambitious and prestigious design contracts for the most discerning clients. Their portfolio is extensive and diverse. Boxwood Design’s accomplishments include residential homes, hospitality, multi-purpose projects, landscape design, ritual baths, assisted living facilities, commercial office space, landscape design, and exterior façade projects completed nationwide. The unifying thread amongst them, impeccable projects delivered with professionalism and honesty. Their commitment to client satisfaction is evidenced by their quick turnaround of projects, completed within budget.

Boxwood Design’s comprehensive service includes complimentary site consultation followed by 3-d drawings, renderings, and Computer Assisted Design that enables collaboration with each respective trade. The relationships they have established with artisans, manufacturers and vendors enable them access to exclusive goods and custom tailored pieces for each project. For innovative, luxurious interior design and space planning, Boxwood Design is the firm you want.
